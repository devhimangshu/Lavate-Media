Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work with startups?

Yes. We partner with ambitious startups that understand the value of a strong brand foundation from day one.

What are your pricing packages?

We offer customized proposals based on your specific goals. However, we also have retainer packages for ongoing SEO and Social Media management.

How long does a branding project take?

Typically 4-6 weeks for a complete identity system (Logo, Guidelines, Typography, Collateral). We value depth over speed.

Do you offer ongoing support after launch?

Absolutely. We provide dedicated maintenance packages to ensure your digital assets remain secure, updated, and performing at peak efficiency long after the initial launch.

How do we communicate during the project?

We believe in transparency. You'll have access to a dedicated project channel (WhatsApp/Slack) and weekly video check-ins with the project lead, ensuring you're always in the loop.

Can you help with content creation?

Yes. Our in-house team handles everything from copywriting to high-end photography and video production, ensuring your brand voice remains consistent across all platforms.
